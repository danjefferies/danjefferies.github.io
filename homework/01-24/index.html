This is one of my favorite photos I've taken in Boston!

When the weather was not yet atrocious, I would go for a walk everyday to the MFA to "sunbathe" in the front lawn.

Funny story...one time I wondered why the grassy area, usually crammed with college students and families alike, was empty. Where were the lawn chairs? Where was I going to sit?

I turned around and the SPRINKLER was on. And boy was I soaked. After that, I decided a nice walk over to the Isabella Stewart Gardner Museum was better suited.

One morning the leaves were nothing but yellow :)
